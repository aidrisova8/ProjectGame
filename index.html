ZARUBA GAME

Timer

    Instructions:

  1. Player 1 starts painting from the top,
    while Player 2 starts from the bottom.
  2. Player 1 clicks the "Start" button.
  3. Player 1 has 10 seconds to color the
    specified number of boxes.
  4. The number of boxes to be colored
    is randomly generated, and players must color
    them either vertically or horizontally based
    on the generated numbers.
  5. Once Player 1 completes their turn, it's Player 2's turn.
  6. If a player can't find a place to color
    within the time limit, they lose.
  7. The game continues until one of the players
    can't find a place to color the specified number of boxes.
  8. Click the "Reset" button to clear the board.
  9. Have fun and may the best artist win!
