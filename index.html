ZARUBA GAME

Timer

    Instructions:

  1. Player 1 starts painting from the top, while Player 2 starts from the bottom.
  2. Player 1 clicks the "Start" button.
  3. Player 1 has 10 seconds to color the specified number of boxes.
  4. Once Player 1 completes their turn, it's Player 2's turn.
  5. If a player can't find a place to color within the time limit, they lose.
  6. Click the "Reset" button to clear the board.
  7. Have fun and may the best artist win!
